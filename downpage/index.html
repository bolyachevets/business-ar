BC Annual Reports Update

BC Annual Reports can no longer be filed on this page. You must now use Corporate Online or the new BC Business Registry.

Where to file your BC Annual Report

If your business is in Corporate Online, use that system to file your annual report. If your business has been moved to the new BC Business Registry, file your annual report there.

Not sure where to go?

The BC Registry application is part of a long-term project to modernize BC Registry and BC OnLine systems. If your business has been moved to the new BC Business Registry, you will have received an email. If you did not receive an email, your business is likely still in Corporate Online.
